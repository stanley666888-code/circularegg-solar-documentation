# Mobile Solar Light Tower - Power Requirements Calculator

## Quick Reference Guide

For those planning to deploy solar lighting systems, here's a practical reference:

### Power Consumption Estimation

**Example Site:**
- Area: 10,000 m² (100m x 100m)
- Required illumination: 30 lux at 10m distance
- Operating hours: 10 hours/night
- Days without sun: 3

**Calculation:**
1. Light tower needs: ~1000W total capacity
2. Battery: 15-20kWh (accounts for inefficiencies)
3. Solar array: 2000W+ (to recharge during day)
4. Duration: 3-5 nights on single charge

### Installation Tips

1. **Ground Preparation**: Ensure flat, stable surface
2. **Solar Exposure**: Maximum sun exposure for charging
3. **Wind Protection**: Secure to prevent tipping
4. **Cable Management**: Protect wiring from damage

### Maintenance Schedule

- **Daily**: Check battery levels, clean panels
- **Weekly**: Inspect all connections, tire pressure
- **Monthly**: Check rotation mechanism, test all functions
- **Quarterly**: Professional inspection and servicing

### Troubleshooting

**Battery not charging:**
- Check solar panel orientation
- Ensure no shading
- Verify battery connections

**Lights too dim:**
- Check battery charge level
- Inspect bulbs/lights
- Clean light covers

**Tower won't rotate:**
- Check motor connections
- Ensure free rotation path
- Verify power source
