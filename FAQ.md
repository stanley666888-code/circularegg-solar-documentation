# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Quick Answers

### General Questions

**Q: How long can a solar light tower run on a full charge?**
A: 3-5 nights of continuous operation, depending on usage intensity and weather conditions.

**Q: What's the lifespan of a solar light tower?**
A: 5-7 years for regular maintenance, up to 10+ years with proper care.

**Q: Do I need to charge it every day?**
A: No. The battery provides several days of operation before requiring recharging, especially during sunny days.

**Q: Can I use it during cloudy weather?**
A: Yes, but with reduced runtime. A full charge will still last 2-3 nights in cloudy conditions.

### Solar Panels

**Q: How much space do the solar panels need?**
A: Each 1,740W system requires about 20 m² of solar exposure. Panels should be placed in direct sunlight without shading.

**Q: Do I need to clean the solar panels?**
A: Yes, we recommend weekly cleaning to maintain charging efficiency. Dust and debris can reduce output by up to 30%.

**Q: What happens if it snows?**
A: Panels will not charge while covered in snow. Clear snow regularly to restore charging capacity.

**Q: Are the panels weatherproof?**
A: Yes, all panels are IP65 rated and designed for outdoor use in all weather conditions.

## Troubleshooting Quick Reference

### Common Issues

**Problem**: No power
- Check battery charge level
- Verify main switch is ON
- Check fuse/breaker

**Problem**: Low light output
- Check battery charge
- Clean light covers
- Inspect bulbs

**Problem**: Not charging
- Check solar panel orientation
- Ensure no shading
- Clean panels

**Problem**: Won't rotate
- Check motor connections
- Ensure free rotation path
- Verify power source
