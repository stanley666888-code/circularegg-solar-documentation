# Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Battery Issues

**Problem**: Battery not charging

**Solutions**:
1. Check solar panel orientation - should face south in northern hemisphere, north in southern hemisphere
2. Ensure no shading on panels (trees, buildings)
3. Verify battery connections are tight
4. Check temperature - extreme cold can reduce charging efficiency

**Status Indicator**: Should show green LED when charging

---

**Problem**: Battery draining too fast

**Solutions**:
1. Check for parasitic loads (unconnected devices)
2. Ensure lights are turned off when not in use
3. Verify battery health - may need replacement
4. Check for night lighting enabled when not needed

---

### Lighting Issues

**Problem**: Lights too dim

**Solutions**:
1. Check battery charge level - low charge = low output
2. Clean light covers and lenses (dust can reduce brightness)
3. Verify bulbs/lights are functioning correctly
4. Check for loose connections

---

**Problem**: Lights won't turn on

**Solutions**:
1. Check battery is fully charged
2. Verify main switch is ON
3. Inspect fuse/breaker - check if blown
4. Test with spare bulbs/lights

---

### Mechanical Issues

**Problem**: Tower won't rotate

**Solutions**:
1. Check motor power source - ensure battery has charge
2. Verify rotation mechanism is free (no obstructions)
3. Check for loose wires or connections
4. Ensure mast is extended and locked in position

---

**Problem**: Tower unstable

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure proper ground preparation (flat, stable surface)
2. Lower mast if windy conditions are high
3. Check tire pressure (if vehicle-mounted)
4. Anchor system if on uneven ground

---

### Weather Issues

**Problem**: System not performing in extreme weather

**Solutions**:
1. For high winds: Lower mast to reduce wind resistance
2. For extreme cold: Allow battery to warm up before use
3. For rain: Ensure all seals are intact
4. For snow: Clear panels regularly

---

### Solar Panel Issues

**Problem**: Low charging

**Solutions**:
1. Clean panels with mild soap and water
2. Check for snow or ice accumulation
3. Verify orientation is correct
4. Check for shading from nearby objects
5. Test panels with multimeter

---
